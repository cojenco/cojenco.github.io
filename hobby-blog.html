Some of my favorite places in Paris

Le Marrais is full of lively energy. It is one the best neighborhoods, in my opinion, to explore by foot and spend a whole day.

La Seine(she/her) is the heart of Paris. If you feel a bit lost in this city. Follow the river and she will show you the best of Paris.

Île aux Cygnes and Parc Monceau are two hidden gems. They are so close to the city yet so quiet and serene.

The best times in Taiwan

Taipei in April is fresh and green. Usually there is occasional rain and humid heat in spring along with wind and breezes. This year, however, i have traveled too much this month to be lucky enough only seeing warm weather and freshly new grown leaves.

Of course these few years have been extremely hot even in early March and April with temperature climbing up to 32 degrees celsius. Still there are plenty of days when you have cozy breezes to enjoy a nice picnic with close friends. A day with 28 degrees celsius is surprisingly comfortable under the shady trees and of course with the company of friends, squirrels, snacks and laughter.
